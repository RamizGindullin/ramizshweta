Our Story

We first saw each other in a hallway — a brief greeting that somehow lingered. A few days later we were seated together at the department Christmas lunch. Conversations that began over coffee and fika quietly became the reason we looked forward to small, ordinary days. By March we called it our first official date. Since then, long walks, shared playlists and easy laughter made it clear: we were building something gentle and true. We can’t wait to celebrate this with you.
